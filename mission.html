Our Mission

Covid-19, the global pandemic has hit everyone hard. The entire country went into an unofficial lock-down and it brought down a disaster to countless people having low-wage job. In response to this situation, under the supervision of our honorable President and Head of the Department Dr. A.K.M Ehtesanul Islam and our Treasurer Tanvir Ahmad Tushar sir, AUST EEE SOCIETY started it's fundraising event Covid-19 : Fundraising for the distressed by AUST EEE SOCIETY on 6th April. Realizing their duty towards the country, all the faculty members, alumni and current students of our department came together to fight this pandemic and help those in need.

We will distribute our funds to the two organizations Lighter Youth Foundation and CHATTOGRAM COVID-19 CRISIS RELIEF for helping out families who cannot afford food as they fail to earn their livelihood. We are also taking measures to reach out more underprivileged families locally.If any individual is willing to help or give any suggestion please feel free to reach us out.
